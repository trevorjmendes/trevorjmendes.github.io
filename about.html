The Author

Short Bio: 21-year-old with experience in both gi and no-gi BJJ, aiming to spread the wonders of Jiu-Jitsu.

Long Bio:This site is created by me, Trevor Mendes. I am a 21 year old living just outside Wilmington, North Carolina where I have been training about Brunson's MMA and Fitness (shoutout Brunson's MMA at Independence Mall), mainly doing their No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu practices. I have been training there for just over three months now, and before that, trained at United Fighting Arts Institute (UFAI) up in Vermont.

I was born and raised in Vermont, and living their for 20 years, I have always dreamed about becoming a star-athlete. Specifically, I have always wanted to be a UFC fighter. After coming to terms with the fact that professional fighters get a lot of brain damage, I resigned to the goal of becoming the best BJJ practioner in the world. Currently, that title belongs to Gordon Ryan.
