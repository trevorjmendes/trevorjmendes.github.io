No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu Guide

Fundamentals

Jiu-Jitsu is a grappling martial art. That means that the techniques will generally involve applying force to the opponent in such a way that they are neutralized without utilizing striking techniques. Basically, Jiujitsu can be boiled down to:

  • Positions
  • Transitions
  • Submissions

You can easily learn this martial art by first understanding these three fundamentals.

Position Control Level Description
Mount High On top of opponent's torso with knees on the ground.
Back Control Very High Behind opponent, chest to back with hooks in (heels hooked across groin).
Side Control Medium-High Top position, chest to chest beside opponent, making a "T".
Closed Guard Medium Bottom position with legs wrapped around opponent.
Half Guard Low-Medium Bottom or top position with one of opponent’s legs controlled.
North-South Medium Top position with chest over opponent’s head.
Turtle None Balled up on all fours.
